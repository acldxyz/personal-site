About

A pursuit of the absurd and truthful

Tech banking at Piper Jaffray led to the buy-side at StepStone, then to Mousse Partners, a family office, where I invested in venture funds and companies. Privateer, an investment I led at Mousse, recruited me to lead strategic finance and complete a spectrum of public and private market transactions. I brought Delta Emerald, a data science-powered VC firm, onto the cap table through a fundraise, and later joined to lead external marketing and internal research.

I became a father in November 2024 and took an extended break to spend time with my son. No other adventure has been as beautiful or absurd. Writing about it at heirlloom.substack.com.

I'm looking for my next role leading private markets for a family office or strategic finance for a growth-stage business.

Privateer Holdings | Principal | Seattle | 2019 – 2022. $60B of annual cannabis spend transitioned from the illicit market to the legal. The complex patchwork of new markets presented an ideal environment for purpose-built tech. Privateer had a portfolio of operating subsidiaries, across which I negotiated two mergers and raised over $100M. I spent most of my time with Leafly, Privateer's three-sided marketplace. I led the finance and data teams and redesigned the monetization strategy, resulting in a 380% lift in GMV and 52% growth in monetized accounts.

Delta Emerald Ventures | Director | NYC | 2022 – 2024. The firm’s intelligence platform ingested $3B in annual transaction data and powered both my research and marketing initiatives. I uncovered a $127B opportunity for legal cannabis, a $30B opportunity for tech, and a consumer migration from alcohol to THC drinks, steering the firm's investment strategy further into software and THC drinks.

Dad | NYC | 2025 – Present. Delta Emerald wound down shortly after my son was born. I took an extended break to care for him. It's been the greatest adventure. A few months into it, I started Heirlloom, a collection of essays and guides that have helped hundreds of new dads.

I'm now searching for my next role, either leading privates for a family office or strategic finance for a growth-stage company. I'm open to select contract/consulting engagements in the interim.
