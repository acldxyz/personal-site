About

Tech banking at Piper Jaffray led to the buy-side at StepStone, then to Mousse Partners, a family office, where I invested in venture funds and companies. I joined Privateer, one of those investments, to lead strategic finance. I worked on a spectrum of public and private transactions and redesigned a marketplace. I brought Delta Emerald, a data science-powered VC firm, onto the cap table through a fundraise. After three years working together, I joined to lead comms and insights.

I became a father in November 2024 and took an extended break to spend time with my son. No other adventure has been as beautiful or absurd. Writing about it at heirloom.substack.com.

I'm looking for my next role leading private markets for a family office or strategic finance for a growth-stage business.
