Month 15: The Fear

What kind of father will I be when Ronan meets adversity?

February 27, 2026 · Adam Dawson

What kind of father will I be when Ronan meets adversity? When he’s bullied for something he can’t control or for who he wants to be? When he has his heart broken, and when he loses something he worked hard for?

Experiences I replay, asking what I could have done differently.

Have I learned enough to help him?
